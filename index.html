It’s evident that the closer a region is to the equator, the hotter it is in comparison to areas that are further away, but what exactly is the difference with the weather, in regards to rainfall? What’s interesting is Victoria having more rainfall than Tasmania, even though Tasmania is closer to the South Pole.
